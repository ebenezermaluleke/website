Project summary

Smart Shopping basket is a new way of making shopping experience fun and the aim is to reduce long queues in shops. For example during month ends or payday there are lot of people in a mall or certain shop becuase customers are buying groceries. Since there are lot of people it create a long queues, and you may find that Bob want to buy single item but he is forced to stand im a long queue, so smart shopping basket reduce long queues because you can scan item into your basket and pay using your cellphone.
